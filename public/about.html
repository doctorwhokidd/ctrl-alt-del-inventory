About Ender Lilies Grimoire

A fan-made companion app to help you track your Ender Lilies journey and collections with ease.

Our Mission

We aim to simplify completion tracking with a lightweight, responsive, and user-friendly interface. Whether you’re a casual explorer or a dedicated completionist, the Grimoire adapts to your playstyle and helps you plan your adventure.

Key Features

  • Unified dashboard for Spirits, Relics, Items, and Findings
  • Track, search, and filter each collection category
  • Progress saved locally for guests, and to your account when logged in
  • Recent activity tracking with undo for tracked/found entries
  • User authentication: sign up, log in, log out, and demo user support
  • Guest mode with temporary progress, persistent progress for logged-in users
  • Responsive design for desktop and mobile
  • Map page for visual reference
  • Notification system for user actions
