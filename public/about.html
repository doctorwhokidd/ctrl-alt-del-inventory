About the Grimoire

About Us

Ender Lilies Grimoire is more than a tracker — it’s a companion for players who want to experience the haunting beauty of Ender Lilies: Quietus of the Knights without losing track of their progress. We built this project to give fans a clear, organized way to monitor their journey through the game’s intricate world, ensuring that every Spirit, Relic, Location, and hidden Finding is accounted for.

Our mission is to simplify completion tracking while keeping the interface lightweight, responsive, and enjoyable to use. Whether you’re a casual explorer or a dedicated completionist, the Grimoire adapts to your playstyle and helps you plan your route through the game’s challenges.

  • Track all major collection categories in one unified dashboard.
  • Save your progress and favorites securely with local storage — no account required.
  • Use category tabs to plan routes, set completion goals, and revisit missed items.
  • Enjoy a responsive interface designed for both desktop and mobile players.
  • Access quick links to community resources, guides, and discussions.
  • Built by fans, for fans — with a focus on clarity, speed, and immersion.
Legends speak of the Grimoire as a living record, a tome that gathers the whispers of fallen knights and the relics they left behind. Each Spirit inscribed within its pages carries echoes of battles long past, while every Relic shines with fragments of forgotten power. To open the Grimoire is to walk alongside Lily’s journey, ensuring that no secret fades into shadow and no memory is lost to time.

The Grimoire is constantly evolving. We listen to community feedback and expand features to make collection tracking smoother and more rewarding. Our vision is to create a tool that feels like part of the game itself — intuitive, atmospheric, and always ready to guide you deeper into the mysteries of Land’s End.

Mission

We strive to make Ender Lilies completion tracking feel natural, immersive, and easy to use. The Grimoire helps players spend more time exploring and less time hunting down what’s missing.

What it offers

Quick progress overviews, category tracking, saved favorites, and a lightweight interface built around the game's atmosphere. No extra accounts are required to get started.
